COSC

We are a collective of ardent proponents of open source software, passionately advocating for its adoption and utilization. As dedicated scholars, we orchestrate informative sessions exploring diverse open source software and cutting-edge technologies, endeavoring to enlighten our students about these invaluable resources while cultivating a fervent passion for their implementation.
